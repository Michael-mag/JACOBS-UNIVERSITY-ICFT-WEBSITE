About

The Intercontinental Football Tournament is an annual, traditional tournament that brings together the different nationalities present in Jacobs University. Members of the university are grouped into their respective continents to bring about an intercultural and international competition. The tournament is more about being a team, regardless of skill and experience, however, the competition is quite stiff.

Please read the RULE BOOK to participate, as violation of the rules is liable to penalty.
